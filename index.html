Cervical Cancer Screening

Founded by Columbia University biomedical engineers, Luso Labs LLC is revolutionizing cervical cancer screening

in low and middle-income countries by coupling existing clinical practices with our cerVIA screening system.

The cerVIA screening system identifies acetowhitened precancerous lesions on the ectocervix. Through a custom

camera system and a unique lesion detection algorithm in a speculum-fitted device, our system addresses critical

problems encountered during visual inspection with acetic acid testing in low and middle income countries.
